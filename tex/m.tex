\tlhw{mevyap}
\tlhd{excl}{Stop, enough already.}
\tlhco{It comes from two Klingon verbs, \tlhe{mev} ("stop, cease") and \tlhe{yap} ("be enough, be sufficient"). No doubt in the past, the locution was longer, perhaps \tlhe{yImev, yap!} ("stop! it\'s enough"). The dropping of the imperative prefix \tlhe{yI-][pe-} is what would be expected in Clipped Klingon. The two words have been used in juxtaposition for so long, they have come to be accepted as a single, though grammatically peculiar, word.}{KGT 113}
